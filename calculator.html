How sleep cycles work

A typical sleep cycle lasts about 90 minutes and includes light sleep, deep sleep, and REM sleep. Waking up in the middle of a cycle leaves you feeling groggy.

This calculator works backward from your target wake-up time (or bedtime) to find times that align with the end of a sleep cycle — so you wake up naturally refreshed.
